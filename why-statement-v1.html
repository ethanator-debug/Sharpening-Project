The Sharpening Project
Why
We Exist

We believe God is calling a generation of young men to rise up — to walk in truth, lead with courage, and find lasting freedom in Christ. Our purpose is to create a Jesus-focused space where young men can be open and real about life — the good, the bad, and the in-between. A place where they can be seen, heard, and deeply loved.

Through honest mentorship, friendship, and discipleship, we help guys find their true purpose in life — to walk in an intimate, loving relationship with God, listening to His voice and growing closer to Him daily.

This is more than a program. It's a movement of brothers sharpening brothers, building strength from the inside out, and learning how to walk in the freedom that only Jesus gives.

"Brothers sharpening brothers — building strength from the inside out."

Our Mission
Three Core Goals
Everything we do flows from these three goals.
1
Loved & Safe

To make sure every student feels loved, valued, and supported — never walking through life alone. A young man grows fastest in a place where he feels safe enough to be honest. We exist to create that place.

2
Lead with Strength

To raise up young men who model faith, humility, and courage — ready to lead and disciple others. Mentors don't just guide students; they become the kind of men students one day want to become.

3
Lasting Freedom

To guide young men toward lasting freedom — helping them walk in truth, overcome temptation, and grow in their relationship with Christ. Freedom from the lies that keep young men stuck.

Everything We Do Flows From This

Every event, meeting, and message filters through our why and these three goals. If it doesn't build love, leadership, or freedom — it doesn't belong here.

Jesus remains at the center. Always.
