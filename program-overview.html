& Purpose

Building Character. Growing Faith. Living Unshaken.

"As iron sharpens iron, so one man sharpens another." — Proverbs 27:17

Brand Story

The Sharpening Project began with a simple conviction: young men today are starving for real guidance, biblical truth, and godly men who will walk beside them. What started as one student's calling from God has grown into a disciplined, scalable, mentorship framework designed to strengthen young men nationwide.

Through intentional one-on-one mentorship, honest conversations, and Christ-centered community, young men learn to replace confusion with clarity, isolation with brotherhood, and fear with unshakable faith.

Every system in The Sharpening Project is built around one core purpose: to raise up strong, humble, Christ-following young men who will lead their generation with courage, integrity, and freedom.

Why Statement

The Sharpening Project exists to help young men walk in their God-given purpose by building a culture of honesty, discipleship, and Christ-centered brotherhood.

We provide a safe place for guys to be real, free from shame, so they can experience lasting freedom from temptation, anxiety, and hidden battles.

Our mission: to guide young men into an intimate, life-transforming relationship with Jesus, teaching them how to hear His voice, follow His ways, and become the men God created them to be.
Three Core Goals
01

Walk in Purpose

Equip young men to discover their identity in Christ and live with clarity, calling, and conviction.

02

Lead with Strength

Train mentors to model faith, humility, courage, servant leadership, and to disciple others the way Jesus did.

03

Find Lasting Freedom

Help young men overcome temptation, break destructive patterns, and build a lifestyle rooted in Scripture, truth, and spiritual maturity.

Movement-Level Vision

To build a reproducible model of Christian mentorship that can operate in any city, any school, any church — raising up Godly young men and transforming generations worldwide.

Program Focus Areas
01 — Focus

One-on-One Mentorship

Weekly, intentional, relational discipleship between a high-school mentor and a younger student.

02 — Focus

Monthly Community Gathering

Large-group hangouts designed for connection, leadership observation, and spiritual devotion.

03 — Focus

Leadership Development Pipeline

A structured system that raises mentors, leaders, and chapter directors who embody Christlike character.

04 — Focus

Safety & Accountability Systems

Bulletproof rules, reporting procedures, training, and documentation to protect all participants.

05 — Focus

Faith Formation & Biblical Discipleship

Rooting every teaching, conversation, and practice in Scripture, prayer, and the power of the Holy Spirit.

Core Values

Christ First

Jesus is the center of this program. Every decision submits to Him.

Integrity Always

We operate with transparency, character, and accountability in all things.

Honor the Family

Parents are the primary spiritual leaders; mentors support, not replace them.

Protection of Students

Safety protocols and reporting procedures are non-negotiable.

Brotherhood

No young man walks alone — mentors or students.

Reproducibility

Everything we build can be multiplied into future chapters with excellence.

The Sharpening Project is more than a mentoring program — it is a movement to restore biblical manhood, strengthen families, and raise up a generation of young men who know who they are and what they were created to do. With Scripture as our foundation, excellence as our standard, and Christ as our leader, we believe this model can transform individuals, schools, churches, cities, and one day, the world.
