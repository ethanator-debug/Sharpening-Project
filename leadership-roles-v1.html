Purpose of This Document

This guide outlines the expectations, requirements, and accountability structure for all leadership roles within The Sharpening Project. Each position exists to build a foundation of strong, Christ-centered leadership and to ensure that the program operates in unity, humility, and excellence.

Every leader holds a unique and vital role — but all roles are bound together by a shared calling: to serve faithfully, lead humbly, and guide others toward freedom in Christ.

Accountability Framework

Overview

The Sharpening Project leadership model is a spiritual web of accountability — where every leader is supported, mentored, and covered in prayer. No leader stands alone; every person is both responsible for others and cared for by someone else.

Structure

Every leader has a dedicated accountability partner. The Head Advisor acts as mentor and accountability partner to all leaders. The Program Director oversees overall flow and reports to the Head Advisor.

"We are a family and a team. Each person is here to help each other as much as they possibly can — whether that's prayer, encouragement, or practical help within the program."
