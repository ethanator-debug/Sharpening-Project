What We Are

The Sharpening Project is a Christ-centered mentorship program where older high school mentors meet weekly with younger students to build faith, confidence, and character through intentional, safe, one-on-one relationships.

No student should walk through life alone. We make sure they never have to.

How It Works

Weekly One-on-One Mentoring

2+ hour meetings. Public or parent-approved locations. Prayer, conversation, encouragement, real-life guidance.

Monthly Community Hangouts

Games, food, friendship, and fun. Parent-friendly events. Short devotional & group encouragement.

Vetted & Trained Mentors

Interviewed, trained, and supervised by adult leaders. Supported through weekly check-ins.

Full Safety Protocols

Your son is always protected, supported, and cared for under our official Rules & Protocols.
